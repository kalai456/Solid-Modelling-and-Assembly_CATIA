# TITLE OF THE PRODUCT
Dust Collector for Air Purifier

# DESCRIPTION
The Dust Collector for Air Purifier is a compact, detachable pre-filtration unit designed to capture coarse dust particles before they reach the primary HEPA and activated carbon filters. The product improves filter life, reduces maintenance costs, and enhances the overall efficiency of air purification systems.

The dust collector is designed using 3DEXPERIENCE CAD tools and manufactured through 3D printing technology. The design incorporates a cyclonic separation chamber that utilizes centrifugal force to separate larger dust particles from the incoming airflow. The collected dust settles in a removable collection chamber, allowing easy cleaning and reuse.

Key Objectives:

1.Improve air purifier filter lifespan.
2.Reduce pressure drop across filtration stages.
3.Develop a lightweight and compact consumer product.
4.Ensure manufacturability through additive manufacturing.
5.Create an aesthetically appealing and user-friendly design.

# CHALLENGES FACED TO DEVELOP

1. Compact Design Constraints

Designing an effective dust collection mechanism within a limited volume while maintaining adequate airflow presented a significant challenge.

2. Airflow Optimization

The internal geometry had to be carefully designed to create sufficient vortex motion for dust separation without causing excessive pressure losses.

3. Structural Strength

The housing needed to withstand handling, assembly loads, and operational vibrations while remaining lightweight.

4. Assembly Compatibility

Proper fit and clearances were required between the collection chamber, housing, filter interface, and locking mechanism to ensure ease of assembly and maintenance.

5. Additive Manufacturing Limitations

The design was optimized to minimize support structures, reduce printing time, and improve print quality.

6. User Maintenance Requirements

The dust collection chamber had to be easily removable for cleaning without requiring tools.

# INFERENCE
A compact Dust Collector for Air Purifier was successfully designed, assembled, analyzed, and prototyped using 3DEXPERIENCE and 3D printing technology. The developed product effectively captures coarse dust particles before they reach the primary filtration system, thereby increasing filter lifespan and improving air purifier efficiency.

The assembly validation confirmed proper fit and functionality, while simulation studies demonstrated adequate structural strength under operational conditions. The prototype validated the feasibility of additive manufacturing for rapid product development.

Future improvements may include Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) analysis for airflow optimization, integration of transparent materials for dust visibility, and the use of engineering-grade polymers for enhanced durability and commercial deployment.
